Disable iTunes from auto-start on iPhone insertion, and auto-sync
1. Launch iTunes
2. iTunes -> Preferences -> Devices: check "Prevent iPods, ..., from syncing automatically"
3. click OK
4. Quit iTunes
5. System Preferences -> Accounts -> Login Items: remove "iTunesHelper"
